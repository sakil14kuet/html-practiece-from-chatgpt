Cutie Cat

sweetest creature in the planet

Cats are graceful, independent mammals renowned for their agility, keen senses, and deep historical bond with humans. As beloved companions, these fascinating animals bring comfort and joy to millions of households worldwide, offering a unique blend of playful curiosity and quiet affection.

Cats have about 473 taste buds, which is far fewer than people (we have 9,000) or dogs (who have 1,700). Cats can taste savory, salty, bitter, and sour flavors, but they don’t taste sweets. But don’t be too sad that
your kitty can’t taste ice cream—as obligate carnivores, they’re happy to stick to eating meat.
